This page has a bit more conversation on the band



'A conversation between Roger Waters and Nick again, 'Nick says''I want to ask you about your own song writing. Do you work at it? Do you sit down and think:- Ah! today i will write a song?'. Roger replies 'Sometimes i do. Sometimes i think RIGHT!, and go and pick up a guitar and occasionally it works out. Usually something just flashes into my mind and i think,well, i better write this down and then i go pick up the guitar its usually a phrase or word or a thought even a idea. Once you've got five words or a series of words that can contain a idea... like "come in here, dear boy" then from that point on it becomes quite easy - or it does to do the one verse' the difficult bit is writing one verse then another and another 'when the first was so easy'.
